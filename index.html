Techpreneur

Enrolling in the Techlar Techpreneur Program will help build and advance your child's tech skills, for an advantage in school and beyond.

Daily Techlars’s Techpreneur Worksheets will take about 30 minutes to complete in two sessions a week at a Techlar Center, and then another five assignments to be completed at home. Your child will work at his or her own pace, mastering each new concept before moving on. With each worksheet, you’ll find your child accelerating important math skills. Many Techlar parents ultimately find their children mastering concepts that put them grade levels ahead of their peers.

Reading Program

The Techlar Reading Program is designed to build the critical reading skills that will help your child develop a lifelong love of reading – and a solid foundation for academic success.

Daily Reading Worksheets will take about 30 minutes to complete – in two sessions a week at a Techlar Center, and another five assignments completed at home. Your child will work at his or her own pace, mastering each new reading concept before moving on. With each worksheet, your child’s overall reading and writing skills will advance incrementally. Many Techlar parents ultimately find their children mastering concepts that put them grade levels ahead of their peers.
